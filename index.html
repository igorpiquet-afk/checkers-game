    📜 How to Play

    • Pieces move diagonally, one square at a time, onto dark squares only.
    • Capture an opponent piece by jumping over it into an empty square beyond.
    • If a capture is available, you must take it (chain jumps continue automatically).
    • Reach the far end of the board to crown your piece a Unicorn King 👑, who can move and capture in any diagonal direction.
    • Win by capturing all opponent pieces or leaving them with no legal moves.
